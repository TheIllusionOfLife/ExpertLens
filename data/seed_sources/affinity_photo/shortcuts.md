# Affinity Photo 2 Essential Shortcuts

## Tools
- V: Move tool | B: Paint brush | E: Erase
- S: Clone stamp | J: Healing brush | P: Pen tool
- M: Marquee selection | L: Freehand selection
- C: Crop | T: Text | G: Fill / Gradient
- Z: Zoom | H: Hand/Pan | I: Eyedropper

## View & Navigation
- Cmd/Ctrl+0: Fit page | Cmd/Ctrl+1: 100% zoom
- Space+drag: Pan | Cmd/Ctrl+scroll: Zoom
- [ / ]: Decrease/Increase brush size
- Shift+[ / ]: Decrease/Increase brush hardness

## Layers
- Cmd/Ctrl+J: Duplicate layer
- Cmd/Ctrl+G: Group layers
- Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+G: Ungroup
- Cmd/Ctrl+click layer thumbnail: Load selection
- Alt+click between layers: Clipping mask
- Cmd/Ctrl+E: Merge selected layers
- Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+E: Merge visible

## Selection
- Cmd/Ctrl+D: Deselect | Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+I: Invert selection
- Select → Refine to clean up hair/fur edges
- Alt+drag selection tool: Subtract from selection
- Shift+drag: Add to selection

## Editing
- Cmd/Ctrl+Z: Undo | Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+Z: Redo
- Cmd/Ctrl+T: Free transform | Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+T: Repeat transform
- Cmd/Ctrl+L: Levels | Cmd/Ctrl+M: Curves
- Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+N: New layer
- Cmd/Ctrl+S: Save | Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+S: Save as

## Personas
- Photo Persona (default): pixel editing
- Liquify Persona: warp/distort
- Develop Persona: RAW processing
- Export Persona: slice and export
